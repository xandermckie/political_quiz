# Political Axes Configuration
# Format: AxisName:Description

Economic:Measures your views on economic policy, wealth distribution, and government intervention in the economy
Diplomatic:Measures your views on foreign policy, international relations, and nationalism vs. globalism
Civil:Measures your views on individual rights, civil liberties, and the balance between freedom and security
Societal:Measures your views on social issues, cultural values, and social progress vs. tradition
